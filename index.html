💥 Why RRM?

40% of food globally gets wasted, and corporations love fake scarcity. The RRM flips the script—empowering people and saving the planet while we’re at it :3

⚙️ How It Works

  • 📦 Donors drop off excess items
  • 🧠 AI scans & sorts goods
  • 📡 Items get sent to places that need them
  • 📬 Delivered via drones, trucks, or community runners

💙 Why It's Awesome

  • 💡 Stops waste & overproduction
  • 👩‍👩‍👧‍👦 Builds community sharing
  • 🌱 Good for the environment
  • 🔥 Tells capitalism to go cry in a corner
  • 🎢 Donaters get benfits too!

📦 "The Books That Changed A Life"

“I donated a box of old textbooks I had lying around from college. A few weeks later, I got a message saying a community learning center in a rural town had used them to start a free tutoring program. I literally cried.” — Jordan, Donor

🧥 "A Coat and a Conversation"

“I found a brand-new coat in the RRM near my neighborhood after losing mine in a storm. It was just my size. There was even a note in the pocket: ‘Stay warm.’ I’ve never forgotten that kindness.” — Samira, Recipient

👟 "Shoes That Ran Across the Country"

“A pair of sneakers I outgrew were scanned into the RRM. Months later, I saw them in a photo online—on the feet of a marathon runner raising funds for cancer research. What the actual heck. 😭” — Milo, Donor

🎮 "Game Night Revival"
